Welcome to TypingTest

This is just a demo of an idea that is available for ages (typing test) that I wanted to implement using current web technologies.

Measuring typing skill

Typing tests measure two things, speed and mistakes, so when you take our typing speed test, do not look only at your speed, look also at the number of your mistakes and concentrate on reducing your mistakes in future tests rather than increasing your typing speed. The end result will be increased productivity.

What does WPM mean?

WPM means "Words per minute" and is based on this calculation: 5 keystrokes equal 1 WPM.

What is a keystrokes?

Every key you hit on the keyboard to type a letter is one keystroke.
